You are imprisoned in a small cell, biding your time. Your first objective, is to escape.
In the cell are various objects that can be combined and used in your daring escape.

Your grandmother has left you a yummy cake. Inside you will find a file that can be USED to destroy the bars in the window.
The window is too high up, meaning you need to devise means of climbing down. Collect X number of bedsheets from your bed. USE them together in the inventory to construct a makeshift rope. use the rope on the window to escape.
